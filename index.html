What is Androge Inu?

Androge Inu ($ANDROGEINU) is aiming to merge the Metaverse and play-to-earn (P2E) sectors, making it one of the best cryptos to hold long-term. Despite only being in stealth launch, it will also soon be audited by (Techrate).

Safety and Security

All people hate rugs except for those who are doing it. In Androge Inu, 100% of its liquidity is locked for a year, and the contract address was renounced. Therefore no one can manipulate tax or disable trading, giving holders safety and new investors trust in the project.

Development and Utilities

The project will be split into different platforms, each of which is packed full of utility. The first one will be the 2D infinite runner, in which players can enjoy fun and thrill at the same time rewards that go with it, second will be the NFT Open-world metaverse, in which players will dive into Androge World to explore its vastness.

Other Platforms

The other platforms in Androge Inu Ecosystem are Androge Inu Swap (a decentralized exchange), Androge Inu Battle Market (an NFT marketplace for in-game assets and art from creators), Androge Inu Battle Arena (an immersive Metaverse world), and Androge Inu Battle Stake (a staking platform that allows users to earn interest on $ANDROGEINU and other cryptos).
